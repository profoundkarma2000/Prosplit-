6-Day Arnold Split Program

Follow Arnold Schwarzenegger's legendary training split focusing on high volume and intensity. This program targets each muscle group twice per week with dedicated push, pull, and leg days.

Training Tips

  • Warm up with 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching
  • Focus on progressive overload - increase weight or reps weekly
  • Rest 48-72 hours between training the same muscle groups
  • Maintain proper form and controlled tempo throughout each rep
