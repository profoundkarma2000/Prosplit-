6-Day Arnold Split

🎯 Key Training Tips

  • Warm up properly before each workout
  • Focus on progressive overload - increase weights gradually
  • Rest 48-72 hours between training the same muscle groups
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper nutrition
  • Perform movements slow and controlled

🏋️ Day 1: Push (Chest, Front Delts, Side Delts, Triceps)

🏋️
Barbell Bench Press
Target your chest, front delts, and triceps with this compound movement.

Form Tips:

Keep feet flat on floor, squeeze shoulder blades together, lower bar to chest with control, press up explosively while maintaining tight core.

4 sets × 6-8 reps
0
💪
Incline Dumbbell Press
Focus on upper chest development with controlled movements.

Form Tips:

Set bench to 30-45°, press dumbbells up and slightly together, squeeze chest at top, control the negative portion slowly.

4 sets × 8-10 reps
0
🤲
Overhead Shoulder Press
Build shoulder strength and stability with strict form.

Form Tips:

Keep core tight, press straight up (not forward), avoid arching back excessively, lower with control to ear level.

4 sets × 8-10 reps
0
🔥
Lateral Raises
Isolate your side delts for broader shoulders.

Form Tips:

Slight bend in elbows, raise arms to shoulder height, lead with pinkies, control the descent slowly, avoid swinging or momentum.

3 sets × 12-15 reps
0
⚡
Triceps Pushdowns
Finish strong with focused triceps isolation.

Form Tips:

Keep elbows pinned to sides, full extension at bottom, squeeze triceps hard, control the weight back up slowly.

3 sets × 12-15 reps
0
🔺
Overhead Tricep Extension
Target the long head of the triceps for complete development.

Form Tips:

Keep upper arms stationary, lower weight behind head with control, stretch triceps at bottom, extend fully at top.

3 sets × 10-12 reps
0

🎯 Day 2: Pull (Back, Rear Delts, Side Delts, Biceps)

🏗️
Bent-over Rows
Build a thick, strong back with this compound pulling movement.

Form Tips:

Hinge at hips, keep back straight, pull bar/weight to lower chest/upper abdomen, squeeze shoulder blades together at top.

4 sets × 6-8 reps
0
📈
Pull-ups/Lat Pulldowns
Develop lat width and upper back strength.

Form Tips:

Pull with lats not arms, chest up and out, pull to upper chest, squeeze shoulder blades at top, control descent.

4 sets × 8-12 reps
0
🎪
Face Pulls
Target rear delts and improve posture.

Form Tips:

Pull rope to face level, elbows high, squeeze rear delts and rhomboids, pause at full contraction, control return.

3 sets × 12-15 reps
0
🔧
Cable Curls
Consistent tension for optimal bicep development.

Form Tips:

Keep elbows at sides, squeeze biceps at top, slow controlled negative, avoid swinging or momentum, full range of motion.

4 sets × 10-12 reps
0
🎳
Single-arm Cable Hammer Curls
Target the brachialis and brachioradialis muscles.

Form Tips:

Neutral grip (thumbs up), keep wrist straight, control both up and down phases, focus on brachialis contraction.

3 sets × 12-15 reps
0

🦵 Day 3: Legs/Abs (Hamstrings, Quads, Calves, Glutes, Abs)

👑
Split Squats
Unilateral leg strength and stability development.

Form Tips:

Keep most weight on front leg, descend until front thigh is parallel, drive through front heel, maintain upright torso.

4 sets × 8-10 reps
0
🎯
Romanian Deadlifts
Target hamstrings and glutes with hip-hinge movement.

Form Tips:

Slight bend in knees, hinge at hips keeping back straight, lower weight towards mid-shin, squeeze glutes and hamstrings to return.

4 sets × 8-10 reps
0
🏃
Walking Lunges
Dynamic movement for functional leg strength.

Form Tips:

Step forward, lower until both knees are at 90 degrees, front knee behind toes, push off back foot to step through.

3 sets × 12-15 reps per leg
0
🚀
Leg Curls
Isolate hamstrings for balanced leg development.

Form Tips:

Lie face down, curl weight towards glutes, keep hips on pad, controlled release, squeeze hamstrings at peak contraction.

3 sets × 12-15 reps
0
🏔️
Calf Raises
Build strong, defined calves with controlled movements.

Form Tips:

Full range of motion, squeeze at the top, slow controlled descent, can be done standing, seated, or on a step.

4 sets × 15-20 reps
0
⭐
Cable Crunches
Core strengthening with added resistance.

Form Tips:

Kneel down, pull cable down towards knees, crunch down engaging abs, control the return, focus on abdominal contraction.

3 sets × 15-20 reps
0
🎪
Hanging Leg Raises
Advanced core exercise for lower ab development.

Form Tips:

Hang from pull-up bar, raise legs with control (straight or bent), engage lower abs, avoid swinging, control descent.

3 sets × 8-15 reps

Hinge at hips, keep back straight, pull cable towards lower chest, elbows close to body, squeeze lats.
